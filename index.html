About Me

I am a MEAL Specialist/Statistician and Director of BM-Takara Consults Ltd, a consultancy firm specializing in research, monitoring and evaluation and data systems strengthening. I bring over five years of progressive experience delivering high-quality evidence across development and humanitarian contexts in Uganda and the wider region.

I have led and contributed to a wide range of baseline, midline, endline evaluations, market systems analyses and impact assessments for international organizations including World Vision, CARE International, Oxfam, IRC, Action Against Hunger, and KOICA.

My technical expertise spans the design of MEAL systems, indicator frameworks, and digital data collection platforms using tools such as Kobo Toolbox, ODK, and R, alongside dashboard development in Power BI.

I am also experienced in capacity strengthening, training teams in data collection, management and analysis using Excel, SPSS, STATA, and R, and translating complex data into actionable insights.
