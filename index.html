About Me

I am a MEAL Specialist / Statistician and Director of BM-Takara Consults Ltd, a consultancy firm specializing in research, monitoring and evaluation, and data systems strengthening. I bring over five years of progressive experience delivering high-quality evidence across development and humanitarian contexts in Uganda and the wider region.

I have led and contributed to a wide range of baseline, midline, and endline evaluations, market system analyses, and impact assessments for international organizations including World Vision, CARE International, Oxfam, IRC, Action Against Hunger, and KOICA. My work focuses on generating actionable insights that inform program design, improve performance, and enhance accountability to beneficiaries and donors.

My technical expertise spans the design of MEAL systems, indicator frameworks, and digital data collection platforms. I am highly skilled in integrating tools such as Kobo Toolbox, ODK, and R to build efficient, real-time data systems, and in developing interactive dashboards using Power BI to support data-driven decision-making.

Beyond analytics, I have strong experience in capacity strengthening, having trained partners and field teams in data collection technologies, data management, and analysis using tools such as Excel, SPSS, STATA, and R. I am passionate about translating complex data into clear, meaningful insights that drive impact at community and policy levels.
