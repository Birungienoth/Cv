About Me

Turning Data Into Actionable Evidence

I am a Data Consultant, Monitoring, Evaluation, Accountability & Learning (MEAL) Specialist and Statistician with extensive experience supporting governments, international NGOs, consulting firms and development partners through research, programme evaluations, statistical analysis and digital data systems.

Who I Am

I currently serve as a Data Consultant at Tax Justice Network Africa (2024 – Present), where I support regional programmes through data analytics, citizen-generated data systems, Power BI dashboards, monitoring frameworks and evidence generation for policy advocacy across Africa.

Throughout my career I have successfully delivered baseline studies, midline reviews, endline evaluations, market systems analyses, impact assessments and monitoring systems for leading development organisations.

My expertise combines advanced statistics, monitoring and evaluation, digital data collection technologies, Power BI dashboards and programme learning to strengthen evidence-based decision making.
